About Me.

I'm Enrique Sanz-Gadea


I am a fifth-year architecture student at the Technical School of Architecture in Madrid, my hometown and current residence. Recently, I completed an exchange year at Seoul National University. In the short term, I plan to pursue an internship abroad and then enroll in a qualifying master's degree program to further my education.

I have always been drawn to creating and immersing myself in my own projects, both in the artistic and engineering realms. I thrive on the challenge of optimizing available resources and solving problems to create something new in the best possible way.

It was no surprise when I discovered my passion for architecture and design during my studies. I chose this career for its combination of art and technology and the opportunity to work with people, things, and ideas. Designing architectural projects is especially exciting for me, as I see every project as a chance to explore new concepts with the aid of technological and computing tools.

I enjoy learning programming and applying it to my work, as it allows me to rethink and reimagine ways of doing things. In fact, I took on the challenge of programming this website myself, creating a personalized virtual space to share with others what I am passionate about and involved in.

My current goal is to continue to educate myself and acquire the tools and opportunities necessary to develop my potential. I believe that we have the possibility to transform the world with our actions and we can aspire to have a positive impact on our environments. To create works that transcend ourselves as authors. I hope to be able to give my best to achieve this.

I'm looking forward to share with you my future projects soon.
